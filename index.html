How to Play

Welcome to the Memory Game! Follow these simple steps to play the game:

  • Click on any card to reveal it.
  • Try to find its matching pair by clicking on another card.
  • If the cards match, they will turn blank.
  • If they don't match, they will flip back over.
  • Match all pairs as quickly as possible to win!

Tip: The fewer moves you make and the faster you complete the game, the higher your score!
